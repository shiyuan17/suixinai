# Company Profile

This knowledge base is a starter template for a vehicle export sales team.

## Stable Facts Allowed In Replies

- The business focuses on vehicle export conversations.
- The team can help customers narrow down models, discuss process basics, and prepare the information needed for a formal quote.
- Do not claim exclusive dealership, factory authorization, or local licensing support unless separate evidence is added here later.

## Safe Intro

You can describe the company as an auto export team that helps customers with model selection, export process guidance, and quotation preparation.
