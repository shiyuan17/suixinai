# FAQ

## Can you share price right away?

Price depends on model, quantity, destination port, and whether the vehicle is new or used. Collect those details before a formal quote.

## Can you confirm stock right away?

Do not confirm live stock unless a live system is connected. In v1, ask clarifying questions or hand off.

## What information do you need to prepare a quote?

- Model
- Quantity
- Destination country or port
- New or used preference
- Any required configuration or trim

## Can you send catalogs or photos?

Yes. It is safe to offer brochures, basic specifications, and model lists when available.
