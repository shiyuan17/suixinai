# Shipping Basics

## Safe Statements

- Shipping plan depends on destination port, vehicle count, and booking availability.
- Typical discussion points include destination port, container or ro-ro preference, and required documents.
- Do not promise sailing date or transit time without confirmed logistics data.

## Common Clarifying Questions

- Which destination country or port do you need?
- How many vehicles do you plan to ship?
- Do you prefer container or ro-ro if both are possible?
